Expert Consultancy Services

Take advantage of our experience for all your project, programme or portfolio management.

How does it work?

  • Consultancy

    At Repton Projects the title of Consultant really means something and that’s why all of our Consultants have at least 20 years’ experience delivering complex Projects, Programmes and Portfolios within a wide variety of commercial sectors. If you require expert, unbiased advice that you can trust Repton Projects offer a wide range of consultancy services including:

  • Project, Programme and Portfolio Management Consultancy

    It’s fair to say that managing important changes to your business can be fraught with danger and therefore requires expert Project Management to ensure success. Repton Projects offers a full Project Management service or tailored consultancy to meet your requirements.

  • Business Process Re-engineering

    Sometimes we don’t know what we are doing wrong until it’s too late. Repton Projects can review your current Project Management processes and provide any necessary improvements.

  • Requirements Gathering Techniques

    The most costly mistakes when undertaking any project is to not fully understand exactly what it is that we are trying to achieve. Our specialist consultants can work with you to develop and document your project’s requirements.

  • Project Management Office

    For companies who run multiple simultaneous projects a Project Management Office can be essential. Talk to one of our consultants and we’d be happy to help you implement one within your organisation.

  • Project Rescue

    If you have a project that is failing to deliver it’s never too late to ask for a little help. We’ve seen it all before.

  • Mentoring

    For both new and experienced managers some projects can be a little daunting. Why not let one of our consultants offer a little guidance when required.
